# Manual Review Guide

Use this guide after automated scans complete.

## Perceivable

- Confirm alt text quality in context.
- Verify captions and transcripts are accurate.
- Check reading order and structure visually and with assistive technology.
- Test PDFs and linked documents separately.

## Operable

- Complete major flows with keyboard only.
- Check Tab and Shift+Tab order.
- Verify visible focus indicators.
- Test dialogs, menus, popovers, and Escape behavior.
- Confirm authentication can be completed accessibly.

## Understandable

- Review labels, instructions, and error messages.
- Confirm errors are programmatically associated.
- Check consistency of navigation and naming.
- Identify cognitive load risks.

## Robust

- Smoke test with NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, or TalkBack where applicable.
- Inspect accessible names, roles, and values.
- Confirm status updates are announced.
- Verify custom controls follow platform patterns.
